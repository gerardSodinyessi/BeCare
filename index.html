Our sectors of activity

Community Manager

Our mission is to create and animate communities in order to develop relationships with your audiences - employees, users, customers... First, we develop a strategy for your company's presence on social media in order to meet the objectives determined in consultation with the various departments concerned (innovation, marketing, communication...). The objectives can be variable: to develop the notoriety and the brand image of your company, to take care of your e-reputation, to set up an internal participative platform to encourage innovation, to deploy knowledge management systems, to open the platform to the external community (consumers, users, general public) in order to allow the co-creation of services, products or content. In concrete terms, we will have to interact with Internet users on the various platforms or social media of your company but also on external sites: blogs, forums, social networks. He/she will be able to propose impactful content according to the objectives to be reached and will know the most appropriate distribution mechanisms. We are also in charge of technology watch (new tools or social media - Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube, LinkedIn ....) but also sectorial (market study). Therefore, he is also the key element to observe the negative feedback and its amplification to the point of becoming a possible crisis. He will therefore be in charge of preventing crisis situations and, as a last resort, implementing crisis management scenarios.

Project management
